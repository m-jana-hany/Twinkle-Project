Pacific Palisades: The Story

The Situation in Pacific Palisades

Listen to the recorded situation report.

In January 2025, Los Angeles woke to chaos, not calm. Winds howled, flames roared, and by nightfall, Pacific Palisades was unrecognizable. Elegant homes, sacred spaces, and even the historic Robert Bridges House vanished into smoke and ash. The city’s lifelines—power, water, communication—failed, leaving thousands stranded in fear. From space, NASA’s satellites revealed the scars: green hills turned into blackened wastelands. It was not just destruction; it was a warning. A reminder that even a city of beauty and wealth is fragile before nature’s wrath. Yet, in the silence after the fire, Los Angeles whispered resilience—vowing to rise again, stronger than before.
